Weekly Workout Plan

Stay on track with your fitness goals!

Monday: Chest & Triceps

  • • Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • • Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • • Cable Flyes: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • • Triceps Pushdowns (Rope or Bar): 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • • Overhead Triceps Extension: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Tuesday: Back & Biceps

  • • Pull-ups or Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • • Bent-Over Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • • Single-Arm Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 10-12 reps per arm
  • • Dumbbell Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps per arm
  • • Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Wednesday: Legs & Shoulders

  • • Barbell Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • • Leg Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • • Romanian Deadlifts (RDLs): 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • • Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • • Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Thursday: Rest or Active Recovery

This is a crucial day for muscle recovery and growth. You can do light cardio (e.g., walking, cycling) or stretching. Avoid lifting weights.

Friday: Full Body or Repeat

You have a few options for Friday, depending on your energy levels and goals.

Option A (Full Body):

  • • Barbell Squats: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • • Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • • Bent-Over Rows: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • • Overhead Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps

Option B (Repeat):

Pick one of the previous workouts (e.g., Legs & Shoulders) and perform it again.

Option C (Rest):

If you feel you need more recovery, take a second rest day.

Saturday & Sunday: Rest

Allow your body to recover completely. This is when your muscles repair and grow stronger. Enjoy your time off!
